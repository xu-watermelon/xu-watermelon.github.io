The first day I write diary

背单词:四级书从14-23

日记:

Diary:

​ Today ,(I decide to write diary everyday)I decide to write a diary every day,because I want to improve my English,so today is the first day,i will persist in it for a long time .

long time .I believe I can do it.Then , I will share my daylife.

​ I had classes for a whole day although today is saturday.Because last term ,we miss the class for two days,so we need to take make-up lessons.

​ I was very tired,I wake up at 8:00 am,I didn’t have time to eat breakfast,so I was hungry for all the morning.But I need to learn program from 18:00 pm to 23:00 pm,I must study hard or I won’t be able to find a job when I graduta from my college.I’m a freshman,I still have many chance to improve myself . Life is full of hope.Come on ,just do it .

  1. 语法错误：
    • “I decide to write diary everyday” 应改为 “I decide to write a diary every day”，“write a diary” 表示写日记，“every day” 是副词短语，分开写。
    • “improve my english” 应改为 “improve my English”，语言类单词首字母要大写。
    • “so, today is the first day” 中，“so” 后面的逗号多余，应去掉。
    • “i will persist it for a long time” 表达错误，可改为 “I will persist in it for a long time”，“persist in” 表示坚持做某事。
    • “on last term” 应改为 “last term”，“last term” 作时间状语，前面不需要加介词。
    • “I was very terried” 拼写错误，应为 “I was very tired”。
    • “I wake up on 8:00 am” 时态错误且介词使用不当，应改为 “I woke up at 8:00 am”，描述过去发生的起床动作，用一般过去时，在具体时刻前用介词 “at”。
    • “I was hungrey” 拼写错误，应为 “I was hungry”。
    • “I will can’t find a job” 表达错误，应改为 “I won’t be able to find a job” 或者 “I can’t find a job”。
    • “when I graduta from my collage” 拼写错误，应为 “when I graduate from my college”。
  1. 改进建议：
    • 丰富词汇和句式，例如描述一天的活动时，可以使用一些连接词，如 “firstly”“secondly”“finally” 等使文章更有条理。
    • 增加细节描述，让日记内容更生动，比如描述课程内容、学习感受等。
  1. 打分：3 分。虽然基本表达出了当天的主要经历和想法，但语法错误较多，词汇和句式比较简单。
